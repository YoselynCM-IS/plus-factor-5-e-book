When you report  
commands

Mom “Sweep the floor.”

Requests:

 

Direct speech: 
Mom “Could you water the plants?” 
(request)

 

Reported speech: 
Mom

me  
to

Direct speech: 
“Don ́t water the plants please.”

not to 
water the plants.
