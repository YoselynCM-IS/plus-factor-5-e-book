Instructive texts

 

An instructive text is a text that instructs or tells you how to do some-

 

thing. When you describe a process, you put the steps in time order.

 

In order to make the steps clear, you should use time order signal

 

words to guide the reader from one step to the next.

 

SIGNAL WORDS

 

first 
second 
more over

 

first of all 
third 
most importantly

 

for one thing 
also 
for example

 

one reason that 
in addition 
finally

 

the next + noun 
another + noun 
the most important + noun
