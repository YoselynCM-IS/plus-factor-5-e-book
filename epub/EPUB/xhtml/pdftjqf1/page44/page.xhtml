What rules the world Beauty or Intelligence?

 

Finding the perfect combination of beauty and brain is not an easy task.

 

If you had both, intelligence and beauty, you would be considered the

 

luckiest person on Earth. But what happens to the millions of people who

 

don’t possess this combination?

 

It is a fact that beauty opens the way to success with its charm and charisma. Beauty

 

not only implies the external look, but also the inner beauty of the soul. Beauty implies

 

captivity, and the world today follows the appearance and the external looks. A famous

 

model wouldn’t have been so successful if she hadn’t looked so well.

 

Image is powerful but it is also superficial. When we talk about a magazine cover with a

 

beautiful girl, beauty becomes a construction that an artist captures, the model is not really

 

her, she is a product that sells something. When we compare, the brain has an advantage

 

over beauty. Intelligence lasts forever and increases more with age, while beauty fades

 

away through time. If a person didn’t have intelligence he or she couldn’t adapt to

 

any situation and win and gain recognition in his or her professional life.

 

Brain undoubtedly has the strong domination to rule the world in comparison to

 

the external beauty. Beauty is a powerful tool which helps you to attract, convince

 

or get what you want. However, if you had been looking for a long-term success,

 

you would have to consider training your intelligence in the first place.
