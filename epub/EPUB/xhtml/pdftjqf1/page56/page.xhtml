Fact...or fake

 

Do you believe in ghosts?

 

Have you ever had a paranormal experience?

 

Have you believed in something that happened  

 

to be false?

 

In this unit you will:

 

•
Learn present perfect simple.

 

•
Learn present perfect progressive.

 

•
Identify and use phrasal verbs.

 

•
Identify and use idioms.

 

Learning outcome

 

•
Identify and use phrasal verbs to express different ideas.

 

•
Express ideas or circumstances occurred in a certain point in the past and still happen in

 

the present.

 

Learning evidence

 

•
Solve a questionnaire that contains phrasal verbs and idioms.

 

•
Students read a paragraph about the activities they have been doing for the last months

 

and produce a conversation with a partner about their written paragraph.
