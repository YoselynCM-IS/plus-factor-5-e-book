Should 
is used to give advice and it can also be used to give opinions.

 

You  
should tell  
your parents your problem.  
You  
shouldn’t 
keep secrets from them.

 

You  
should have 
your hair cut; it is too long.

III.  
Read these real problems and write in your notebook a piece of advice to each

 

of them. What should they do? Support your answer.

 

May 28, 2019

 

Hello! My name is Sue, and I am a shy girl. It might be my name; it might be I’m

 

not that sociable. I have a few friends and I can count them on the fingers of my

 

hand; but when it comes to boys, I’m not that lucky. I wish I had more friends. I’d

 

love to change my personality, but I don’t know how. Please, help me.

 

Solitary, 15

 

July 19, 2019

 

I’ve been through a lot over the last few years. I’ve had bullying-related issues for

 

as long as I can remember, but now, it has been getting worse! My classmates

 

laugh at me, hit me, steal from me, destroy my notebooks, and so on. I also have

 

problems with cyber-bullying. I feel depressed, and the usual advice, “ignore it”

 

doesn’t work. I can’t put up with this situation anymore. Please, help me.

 

Bullied, 16
