In this unit you will:

 

•
Learn modals of probability, obligation, prohibition, deduction  

 

and concern.

 

•
Learn to give advice (use of should).

 

•
Listen for specific information.

 

•
Answer questions about main ideas.

 

•
Follow a sequence and write a list of rules.

 

Learning outcome

 

• Recognize and produce sentences about obligations, piece of advice, predictions and

 

concerns in both oral and written forms, based on informative texts such as newspapers

 

and science books.

 

Learning evidence

 

• A short conversation with their classmates to give a piece of advice.

 

• Written sentences about something they are worried about.

 

• A list of rules for a particular place (museum, hospital, school, etc.).
