Expected Learning: 
Identify a descriptive text.

 

WRITING:

 

Descriptive text

 

In a descriptive text, you give a clear, detailed picture of one person, place, thing or event.

 

Tips to write descriptive text:

 

1.
Choose a meaningful person, place, or thing.

 

2.
Introduce the person, place, or thing you are describing.

 

3.
Describe smells and tastes if you can.

 

4.
Describe how the moment or item feels.

 

5.
Make unique observations

 

6.
Include some figurative language.
