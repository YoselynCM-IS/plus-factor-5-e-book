GRAMMAR 
:
Reported Speech

 

We use  
reported speech 
when we want to repeat what someone previously said.

 

In
reported speech 
the tenses, pronouns and word order may be different.

 

Look at these examples:

 

Direct speech: 
“I love music.” 

 

Reported speech:  
He said that he loved music.

 

Direct speech: 
“They worked for this company for ten years.”

 

Reported speech:  
She  
told 
me (that) they had worked for the/that company for ten years.
