Personal and impersonal passive

 

They are used to express other people’s opinions. 
Common verbs

 

When we do not know or want to mention the identity of the agent. 
used in this structure:

 

Active voice: 
People say 
that children are afraid of ghosts. 
say, believe, know,

 

Impersonal passive:  
It is said that 
children are afraid of ghosts. 
think, report, expect,

 

It + passive + that + clause. 
assume, claim,

 

Personal passive:  
Children are said to 
be afraid of ghosts. 
predict, allege.

 

subject + passive + to-infinitive
