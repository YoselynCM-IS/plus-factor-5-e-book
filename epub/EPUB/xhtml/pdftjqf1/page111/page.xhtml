Present simple to talk about the future

 

The  
present simple  
is used to refer to events i 
n the future 
which are certain because they are

 

facts 
, or because there is a clear or fixed  
schedule 
or  
timetable 
.

 

Examples:

 

She  
has
an English test next week. (a fixed arrangement)

 

The plane  
arrives 
at 10:15. (a timetable)

 

What time does your train to Vancouver  
leave 
? (a schedule)

 

Their anniversary  
falls 
on Saturday next month. (a known fact about the future)
